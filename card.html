Giraffe: Spot of the Savannah

Head and Shoulders Above All

You certainly know that the giraffe is the tallest animal in the world, but did you know that the giraffe needs just about five to 30 minutes of sleep in a day? You certainly wish you were a giraffe right now!

  • Scientific Name: Giraffa
  • Average Length: 18 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: Savannah

There are many more reasons to be a giraffe for a day. It can consume up to 45kg of leaves in a day yet it remains sticky thin, can drink water once in several days and yet not be dehydrated, gives birth standing up - oh how regal - and lives up to 25 years! So next time you are told to eat your veggies, be like the giraffe and you will grow up strong and healthy.
